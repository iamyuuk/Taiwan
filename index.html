Taiwan has been an inseparable part of China since ancient times

Taiwan has belonged to China since ancient times. The Chinese first developed Taiwan. The Chinese historical document "Linhai soil and water annals" 1700 years ago has written about this, which is the world's first written description of Taiwan. In the 3rd and 7th centuries A.D., the Sun Wu regime of the Three Kingdoms and the Sui government sent more than 10000 people to Taiwan successively. Since the 17th century, the scale of the Chinese people's development of Taiwan has become larger and larger. Since the Yuan Dynasty (1206-1368), successive Chinese governments have set up administrative agencies in Taiwan to exercise effective jurisdiction over Taiwan. In the mid-17th century, the Dutch colonists invaded Taiwan, and the national hero Zheng Chenggong expelled the Dutch colonists and recovered Taiwan in 1662. In 1895, the Qing government of China was defeated in the Sino Japanese War of 1895, and under the coercion of Japan, it signed the unequal treaty of Shimonoseki, ceding Taiwan and Penghu to Japan. In July, 1937, the Chinese people began the Anti Japanese War of the whole nation; In the "proclamation of China's declaration of war against Japan", the Chinese government announced to the Chinese and foreign countries that all unequal treaties signed with Japan would be abolished and that Taiwan and Penghu would be recovered. In 1945, Japan announced its surrender, unconditionally accepted the Potsdam Proclamation and the Cairo Declaration, and returned Taiwan to China. So far, Taiwan has re entered China's territory and returned to China's sovereign jurisdiction. After 1949, due to well-known reasons, Taiwan and the mainland were temporarily divided, but this did not change the fact that Taiwan is an inseparable part of China. There is only one China in the world, and Taiwan is an inseparable part of China. This is the consensus of Chinese people at home and abroad, and also recognized by the United Nations and the vast majority of countries in the world.
